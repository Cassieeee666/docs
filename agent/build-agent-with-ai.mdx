title: "Build Agent with Vibe Coding Builder"
---

Solvea uses **AI to automatically create and refine your agent**. \
Simply describe your business needs in plain language, and Solvea AI will generate an agent with the appropriate role, tools, and behavior.

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Discovery page">
    After signup, go to **Discovery** to start creating an agent.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-121.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Needs">
    Enter your request, for example:\
    **“ Build an AI MedSpa receptionist to answer treatment FAQs, manage bookings, and handle customer inquiries via email and WhatsApp.”**

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-122.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Knowledge">
    Simply enter your needs along with the website URL you want the AI to learn from. The AI will automatically extract content from the webpage and add it to the knowledge base.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-123.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer Follow-up Questions">
    During setup, Solvea asks clarifying questions to complete the configuration.

    - Creation progress is shown in real time.
    - If requirements are unclear, AI will ask qustions to fill  in the gaps.
    - If a channel is unsupported, AI suggests alternatives.

    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Card>
        Creation progress is shown in real time.

        <Frame>
          ![Image](/images/image-133.png)
        </Frame>
      </Card>
      <Card>
        Answer the AI’s questions to drive the creation process.

        <Frame>
          ![Image](/images/image-135.png)
        </Frame>
      </Card>
    </Columns>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Refine">
    Review the agent’s personality,functions, and sample conversations.\
    Enter additional instructions to adjust the agent if needed.

    <Frame>
      ![Image 124](/images/image-124.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Continue">
    Click **"Looks good"** to proceed to the Agent configuration page.

    <Tip>
      For the configuration, please refer to [Configure your Agent](/agent/configure-your-agent).
    </Tip>
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-130.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
