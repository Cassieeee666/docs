You can train and test your **Agent** or **Skill** from the \*\*Playground \*\*on the right side of the **Agent** or **Skill** page. This allows you to continuously refine how your AI agent understands instructions and executes tasks.

## Training

Training refers to improving agent behavior by chatting with the Solvea AI and providing additional instructions. The AI uses these instructions to adjust configurations and execution logic.

### Training Scope

| **Training on the Agent page** | Updates the agentâ€™s overall configuration, including all associated skills. |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Training on the Skill page** | Updates only the configuration of the selected skill.                       |

### Training Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Training** and enter your instructions in the input box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Respond to clarification questions generated by the AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Preview the updated agent configuration and review the required checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Complete the checklist steps as instructed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Publish** to apply the changes and activate the agent.

    <Note>
      Changes will take effect only after you Publish the agent or skills.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
