Statistics provide a high-level view of your Agent’s conversation volume and resolution performance.

<Frame>
  ![Analytics](/images/analytics.png)
</Frame>

## Time Range

Select a time range to view data (default: last 7 days).

All metrics are recalculated in real time based on the selected period.

## Overall Metrics

- **Total Conversations**\
  The total number of customer conversations handled by the AI Agent during the selected period.
- **Conversations Handled by AI**\
  The number of conversations resolved without being transferred to a human agent.
- **AI Resolution Rate**\
  The percentage of conversations resolved by the AI Agent\
  _(AI-handled conversations ÷ total conversations)_

## Channel Overview

Displays the number of conversations handled each day within the selected time range.\
Each bar represents the total daily volume, aggregated across all channels.
