title: "Topic Insights"
---

Topic Analysis highlights the types of requests your AI Agent could not resolve.

Solvea clusters all conversations that were transferred to human agents during the selected period and groups them by topic, along with the number of occurrences.

## Improve Knowledge from Topics

You can turn unresolved topics directly into knowledge entries:

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select a topic that needs improvement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Add Knowledge**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Enter the correct answer and refine the question in the pop-up window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Confirm** to automatically add the content to the Knowledge Base.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This allows you to continuously improve AI performance based on real customer demand.
