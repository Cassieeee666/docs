title: "Example: Gmail Authorization"
---

This guide uses Gmail as an example to demonstrate three supported email authorization methods.

Other email providers follow similar processes, but specific steps may vary. Please refer to your email provider’s official documentation if needed.

## API Authorization

This is the easiest and most secure way to connect Gmail.

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Continue with Google**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    You will be redirected to Google’s authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Complete the Authorization
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Forwarding Authorization

This method allows Solvea to receive emails via Gmail forwarding.

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Open Gmail and go to **Settings**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image 7](/images/image-7.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **See all settings**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image 8](/images/image-8.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Navigate to **Forwarding and POP/IMAP**. Click **Add a forwarding address**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image 9](/images/image-9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Copy the forwarding email address provided in Solvea and paste it into Gmail.

    <Frame>
      ![Image 10](/images/image-10.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Save the forwarding configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## IMAP / SMTP Authorization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable IMAP in Gmail">
    - Open Gmail and go to **Settings** → **See all settings**.

      <Frame>
        ![Gmailseeallsettings](/gmailseeallsettings.png)
      </Frame>
    - Navigate to **Forwarding and POP/IMAP**.
    - Enable **IMAP Access** and Click **Save Changes**.

      <Frame>
        ![Startimap](/startimap.png)
      </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Google Two-Step Verification">
    - Go to your Google Account Security Center:\
      https://myaccount.google.com/
    - Open **Security** settings and find **2-Step Verification**.

      <Frame>
        ![2stepsverification](/images/2stepsverification.png)
      </Frame>
    - Enable two-step verification.

      <Frame>
        ![Enablestepverification](/images/enablestepverification.png)
      </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate an App Password">
    - In the Security page, search for **App Passwords**, or visit:\
      https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
    - Enter any name for the app and Click **Create**.

      <Frame>
        ![Createapp](/images/createapp.png)
      </Frame>
    - Copy the generated app password.

      <Frame>
        ![Generatedapplicationpassword](/images/generatedapplicationpassword.png)
      </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete IMAP / SMTP Setup">
    - Return to the Solvea email authorization page.
    - Paste the generated **App Password** into the authorization field.
    - Click **Verify** to complete the setup.

      <Frame>
        ![Setimap](/images/setimap.png)
      </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
