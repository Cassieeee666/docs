---
title: "Manage Phone Numbers"
---

In the phone number list, you can manage each number and control its capabilities.

- Enable or disable **Calls**
- Enable or disable **SMS**

All successfully added phone numbers support **Calls** by default.\
**SMS requires additional configuration**, depending on the number type.

## Configure SMS Capability

Once configured, you can enable SMS in the Solvea phone number settings.

### Solvea-purchased numbers

<Info>
  SMS capabilities will be **enabled by default** for all newly purchased phone numbers soon. Stay tuned!
</Info>
