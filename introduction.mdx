## W1How Solvea Works

Solvea connects conversations, skills, and human agents into one intelligent workflow—so customer requests are handled efficiently, accurately, and at scale.

- Customers reach out through any channel.
- Solvea analyzes intent and selects the best skill.
- The AI resolves the request or escalates to a human agent.
- Human agents step in when needed and close the loop.
- Insights continuously improve Solvea’s performance over time.
