## Shopify

After authorizing your Shopify store, you can sync product-related knowledge into a specified folder.

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select the folder where the knowledge should be stored.\
    Click **Add Knowledge** → **Shopify**
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    You will be redirected to the **Deploy** module to complete the setup.

    <Tip>
      For detailed instructions, refer to the [Shopify integration guide](/deploy/e-commerce-platform/shopify).
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Zendesk

Sync articles from your Zendesk Help Center into the knowledge base.

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select the folder where the knowledge should be stored.\
    Click **Add Knowledge** → **Zendesk**
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Enter your Zendesk domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Sync** to start importing content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Salesforce

Import knowledge articles from Salesforce.

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select the folder where the knowledge should be stored.\
    Click **Add Knowledge** → **Salesforce**
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Enter your Salesforce domain and API credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Authorize and Import** to start syncing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
