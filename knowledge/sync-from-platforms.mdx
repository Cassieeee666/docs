## Shopify

After authorizing your Shopify store, you can sync product-related knowledge into a specified folder.

<Info>
  Product knowledge synced from **Shopify** will be **automatically updated daily**.
</Info>

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select the folder where the knowledge should be stored.\
    Click **Add Knowledge** → **Shopify**

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-33.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    You will be redirected to the **Deploy** module to complete the setup.

    <Tip>
      For detailed instructions, refer to the [Shopify integration guide](/deploy/e-commerce-platform/shopify).
    </Tip>
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-34.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Zendesk

Sync articles from your Zendesk Help Center into the knowledge base.

<Info>
  Knowledge synced from **Zendesk** will **not be auto-synced daily**. If there are updates, you will need to re-sync manually.
</Info>

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select the folder where the knowledge should be stored.\
    Click **Add Knowledge** → **Zendesk**

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-35.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Enter your Zendesk domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Import** to start importing content.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-36.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Salesforce

Import knowledge articles from Salesforce.

### Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    Select the folder where the knowledge should be stored.\
    Click **Add Knowledge** → **Salesforce**

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-37.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Enter your Account URL, Client ID and Client Secret.

    <Note>
      To obtain your Client ID and Client Secret, please refer to the official Salesforce documentation: [Generate an API Client ID](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=cc.b2c_generate_api_client_id.htm&type=5).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="">
    Click **Identify** to start syncing.
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-38.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
