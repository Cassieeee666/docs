title: "Outbound Overview"

Solvea supports **AI-powered outbound calling**, allowing your AI agent to proactively call customers and handle conversations automatically.

This feature is ideal for scenarios such as:

- Sales outreach
- Appointment reminders
- Customer re-engagement
- Surveys and feedback collection
- Payment reminders and renewals

Once enabled, your AI agent can **initiate calls and interact with customers at scale**, saving significant manual effort.
