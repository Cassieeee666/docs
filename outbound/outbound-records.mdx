title: "Outbound Records"
---

Solvea provides detailed tracking for every campaign and call.

## Campaign Overview

In the **Outbound module**, you can view:

- **Campaign Name**
- **Call From Number**
- **Status**
- **Recipients** (successful calls)
- **Failed Calls**
- Call breakdown:
  - Sent
  - Picked Up
  - Completed
- **Last Sent Time**

### Actions

- Click the **Failed** count to download failed numbers for retry

## Call Logs

Click **Call Log** on the right side of a campaign to view detailed records in **Inbox**, including:

- Individual call history
- Conversation details
- Call outcomes
