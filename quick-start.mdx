# Step 1: Create an AI Agent with AI

Use natural language to generate your AI receptionist by chatting with Solvea AI.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Navigate to Discovery" icon="telescope">
    After signup, go to **Discovery** to start creating your agent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Describe Your Agent" icon="user-robot">
    Enter your requirements in the chat box.

    _For example: “Create an AI receptionist for my hotel to handle bookings and service inquiries.”_
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Answer Follow-up Questions" icon="comment-question">
    The AI will ask questions during the process. Follow the prompts and chat with the AI to finalize the Agent's creation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review and Confirm" icon="check">
    Preview the agent’s personality, core skills, and sample conversations, then click\*\*“Select” \*\*to enter the Agent page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>


# Step2: Configure Skills

Activate skills by completing required integrations.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Identify Required Skills" icon="sparkles">
    Skills that need setup are clearly marked. Click on the Skill to enter the configuration page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connect Channels and Tools" icon="sparkles">
    Click **Connect** and follow the instructions to authorize integrations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
