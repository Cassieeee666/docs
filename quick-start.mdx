In just five minutes, you’ll transform your ideas into a fully functional AI Agent, ready to handle bookings and inquiries with human-like precision. Let’s bring your AI workforce to life!

# Overview

Here's what you will accomplish in this guide:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Agent from Templates">
    Solvea provides pre-built agent templates based on common business scenarios
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Skills">
    Connect the required channels and tools for the generated skills.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Train">
    Use the playground to test conversations and let AI optimize the setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a Phone Number and Go Live">
    Get a free 7-day phone number and start handling real calls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

# Step 1: Create an Agent from Templates

Choose one pre-built agent templates based on your business scenarios

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Navigate to Discovery" icon="telescope">
    After signup, go to **Discovery** to start creating your agent.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-59.png)
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Select a Template" icon="people-group">
    Click **Use template** to enter the Agent configuration page and start customizing.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-60.png)
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Step2: Configure Skills

Activate skills by completing required integrations.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Identify Required Skills" icon="wrench">
    Skills that need setup are clearly marked. Click on the Skill to enter the configuration page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connect Channels and Tools" icon="link-horizontal">
    Click **Connect** and follow the instructions to authorize integrations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Step3: Test and Train

Validate performance and improve responses.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Test in Playground" icon="circle-play">
    Chat with your AI agent in the Playground Test Area.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-61.png)
    </Frame>
    As you test, Focus on:

    <Check>
      Accuracy: Whether the agent’s responses are correct.
    </Check>
    <Check>
      Relevance: Whether responses are relevant to user question.
    </Check>
    <Check>
      Completeness: Whether the agent follows all defined steps and completes the workflow.
    </Check>
    <Check>
      Tone: Whether the agent’s tone matches your expectations.
    </Check>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Refine your Agent" icon="sparkles">
    You can adjust the Agent configuration based on your test results:

    - Modify the Agent’s **role, tone, and global rules** in the **Persona** section.
    - Set up  **greeting**.
    - To modify the task workflow, make changes in the corresponding **Skill**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-62.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      For detailed instructions, please refer to: [Configure your Agent](/agent/edit-profile), [Create a Skill from Scratch](/skill/create-new)
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Step4: Go Live

Start handling real conversations.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Get a Phone Number" icon="phone">
    A free phone number valid for 7 days is automatically assigned to your agent, which will automatically handle all incoming calls.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-63.png)
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Start Using Solvea" icon="headset">
    **Publish** the Agent to take it live. 

    Then call the number to experience real conversations. Use it across any business channel to handle inbound calls!
