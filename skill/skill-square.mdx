Solvea provides ready-to-use skills covering industries such as _e-commerce, hotel, and service businesses_.\
Popular skills include _product recommendations, knowledge-based Q&A, appointment scheduling, and logistics tracking_.

You can quickly find and add suitable skill templates based on your business needs.

## Find a Skill Template

- From the Agent page, click **Add New Skill** to open the skill creation view and access Skill Square.
- You can browse skills by industry.

  Templates are grouped into categories such as **E-commerce**, **Hotels**, **Food Service**, and **Auto Repair**, making it easier to find relevant skills.

## Enable a Skill Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview">
    Hover over a skill template and click **Preview**.

    In the preview, you can review:

    - Skill type (email, chat, or inbound call)
    - Required tools and platforms
    - Skill description and intended use cases
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add">
    If the skill fits your needs, click **Add** to attach it to your agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    After clicking **Add**, you will be directed to the Skill configuration page. Please refer to [Create a Skill from Scratch](/skill/create-new) - Step3/Step4 guide to configure your Skill.
  </Step>
</Steps>
